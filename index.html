Greenthumb Therapy

An app for finding compatible plants.

Although plants are a fundamental part of nature, taking care of them and even figuring out where to start can be difficult to the regular person. Utilizing scientific data off of Trefle, Greenthumb Therapy allows its users to look up plants through a questionnaire or search. Users can then save plants that they either like or keep track of plants that they wish to weed out of their search. Once they figure out what plants they are looking for, they can click on the links at the bottom of the plant card for more information about a plant and possibly where to find the plant.

Gimme Books

An app to search for books and get information about them.

Due to the sheer number of books in the world, it can be daunting to find a book to read. Gimme Books leverages the Google Books API to help users find a book by author or title. Users who sign up for an account can also view a book recommendation list to assist them in their search to find the next book to read. Additionally, users can save books to their Book List, which helps keep track of their favorite books.

Eat Da Burger

An app for ordering and eating burgers.

It's no doubt that burgers are popular in America as a go-to food. With Eat Da Burger, it is easy to send a request burger to eat and choose a person to eat it. Once a burger has been eaten, the burger name and the person who ate it are listed so people can see who eats the most burgers and what burgers are the most popular.

Bring Your Own Diet

A website for finding recipes and restaurants.

For foodies and frequent travelers, it can be difficult to find food that you enjoy. For those that enjoy cooking at home, you can use the recipe search for finding a recipe from anywhere in the United States. Multiplayer Rock-Paper-Scissors

A website for playing Rock-Paper-Scissors online.

While rock-paper-scissors is traditionally played between two people, this website facilitates the game between two players over the internet. Although only two players can play, any other users can watch the game or be put on the site's wait list until one of the players leave the game. All players can access and post in the chatbox during the game. The chatbox also has a bot that announces when the players have made their moves as well as when someone wins.
